## 2025-05-11 - [Aria labels for icon-only buttons]
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons (e.g., ✕, ✓, ✗, 🗑, ↺) are inaccessible to screen readers without descriptive text. Adding `aria-label` and `title` attributes significantly improves accessibility and usability.
**Action:** Always include `aria-label` for buttons that do not have visible text labels.
